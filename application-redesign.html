Terminal – Redesigning the Candidate Application Experience

Terminal was hyper-focused on increasing their supply of top-quality full-time remote engineers in 2022 to be matched with jobs posted by their US based customers. The company was seeing many applicants coming directly from postings on Linkedin, however, among those applications, only 1/10 engineers would create an account with us, preventing those users from being matched with other potential job openings that may have a higher likelihood of landing.  

Growing Our Candidate Supply 

Working alongside my Growth Product Manager and a team of 3 engineers, my role as Product Designer was to seek a way to increase the number of Terminal accounts created from candidates applying directly from LinkedIn.

Looking At The Bigger Picture

When we knew we had to redesign the application experience, we took a step back and mapped out the existing experience, identified areas of opportunity, and collaborated with Engineering to understand the different authentication flows needed.

Changes Made With Our Candidates In Mind

Pain Point

Candidates are overwhelmed with one long form as the first step

We created a simplified form experience to help candidates focus on one step at a time

Pain Point

Candidates didn’t understand why they would create an account with Terminal for a one-time job application

We guided candidates to create an account with the promise of what they get out of their account with Terminal instead of asking for sign up before their application submission

Problem

Candidates that applied for jobs in the past, were spending unnecessary time re-filling our form and creating duplicate accounts

We helped candidates that already applied to Terminal jobs in the past, claim their account instead of having them fill out the form again

Problem

Candidates submitting an application were unclear of what to expect next

We helped candidates know what the next steps are for their applications with a personalized success message for extra delight

Results

With the redesign, candidates more quickly completed their application form, and felt more confident in what Terminal was offering by creating an account. 

In just a few days, we saw a massive jump (approx. +88%) in the number of new candidates creating an account with Terminal and exploring the Candidate Portal.
