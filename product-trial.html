We presented our insights and product-led growth recommendations to leadership. One key finding from our research and workshops was the need for customers to quickly assess candidate quality. Designing Our First Product-Led Customer Experience

Website Updates

We removed the original contact form for customers and added new entry-points to the Product Trial and a new Offerings page to support our new experience.

New Customer Registration Flow

Our original flow asked users 9 questions to complete their customer profile before entering the free trial. After testing and aligning with Sales about what could remove, we reduced the flow to 4 questions - less headache for our users, higher conversion for the business!
