Chimney.ai – Designing a Personalized Onboarding MVP

Chimney, a property tech startup, needed a way for potential home buyers to find their perfect homes more accurately. The result: a new onboarding initiative designed to personalize home listings shown to buyers.

How can we help home buyers find quality homes faster and more accurately?  

That was the question that kicked off Chimney’s onboarding initiative. Currently, most property search websites lean on simple filters to help home buyers find properties, leading buyers to feel overwhelmed with too many options.

In order to get home buyers to spend less time browsing and matched with properties they will like faster, Chimney required an onboarding that could help us understand the buyer’s profile.


Early Insights

To understand the design challenge better, we discussed what type data was required from buyers to make our property recommendation algorithm more accurate. We also ran user interviews with home buyers to understand their motivations and buying process better.

Collaborative Design

Since this part of the product had a huge impact on the rest of the experience, it was essential to make sure each team had a voice in the design process and remained informed of Design’s thinking.

Introducing Chimney’s Personalized Home Buyer Onboarding

The new onboarding experience is currently in Beta as of July 2023 to start testing with real home buyers.

Impact

The new onboarding has been released as a Beta, and we expect that the shorter flow will increase the likelihood that new buyers will complete their profile and generate quality results on the property listing recommendation page.

It has also helped Chimney gain a better understanding of our users and helped shape future phases of the product.
